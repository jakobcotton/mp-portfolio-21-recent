Welcome to my photography portfolio.

Kill the Bill Protest - 03/04/2021

Protestors Gather

In this photograph, I wanted to capture the magnitude and chaos of the event. There were lots of people attending the protest. The atmosphere was initially quite tense and I was afraid the situation was going to escalate and the police would come to disperse the crowd. Interestingly, that did not end up happening despite the ongoing "rule of six" restrictions.

End Police Violence

I wanted to get a shot from the back of the protestors holding up their signs. I found the "end police violence" sign particularly interesting, with its bright colours and stark messaging.

Listening To the Speaker

At my vantage point atop the steps of the Manchester Central Library, I wanted to get a shot of the crowd listening one of the speakers. I find this shot interesting because it is obvious that the speaker is very passionate about his subject. He was walking around, gesturing as he projected his voice to the crowd of eager listeners.

The System Isn't Broken, It Was Made That Way

I wanted to take a picture with this red sign in it. The message on it is very thought provoking and made me stop in my tracks whilst I contemplated its meaning. I also found the sign belonging to the woman in the bottom right to be very provocative and quite humorous.

A Tram Passes

I find this picture quite powerful because of the contrast between the tram (normal city life) and the protestors (civil unrest).
